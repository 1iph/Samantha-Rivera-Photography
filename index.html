About Me

Hi! I'm Samantha Rivera, a passionate photographer with a love for capturing moments. My journey in photography began at a young age, and it has since evolved into a lifelong passion.

I specialize in portrait and landscape photography, and I strive to tell stories through my lens. With an eye for detail and a commitment to excellence, I work with clients to create stunning images that they'll cherish forever.

When I'm not behind the camera, I enjoy hiking in nature, traveling to new destinations, and finding inspiration in the world around me. I believe that photography is not just about taking pictures—it's about preserving memories and emotions.
